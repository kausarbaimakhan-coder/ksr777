Dastan

Dastan Daribayuly Orazbekov – Kazakh Singer, Dancer, and Actor Born: February 5, 1982 Birthplace: Zhanatas, Sarysu District, Jambyl Region, Kazakhstan Dastan Orazbekov is a prominent Kazakh artist known for his multifaceted talents as a singer, dancer, and actor. He gained widespread recognition as a lead member of the popular music group Orda. Musical Journey: In 2000, Dastan joined the "Bauyrjan-Shou" theater, where he became part of the Orda troupe. The group gained fame for their dynamic performances, blending traditional Kazakh music with contemporary elements. Dastan's powerful vocals and stage presence contributed significantly to the group's success. Solo Endeavors: Beyond his work with Orda, Dastan embarked on a solo career, releasing hit songs such as "Ayttim Salem, Qalamqas" and "Tumarim." His music resonates with a wide audience, showcasing his versatility and emotional depth. Television and Acting: Dastan expanded his artistic repertoire by participating in television shows. Notably, he served as a coach on the second season of "The Voice of Kazakhstan Kids," mentoring young talents and sharing his expertise.

Early Life and Career:

  1. From a young age, Dastan was immersed in the arts, engaging in singing and dancing during his school years. His passion for performance led him to pursue a career in entertainment.

Personal Life:

  • Dastan is married to Janat, and they have two children;Aldiyar and Demirel.
  • Despite his public persona, he maintains a private family life.
